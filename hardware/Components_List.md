## Components Used

### Control and Drive
- ESP32 Development Board  
- L298N Motor Driver Module  
- DC Gear Motors (4)  
- 4-Wheel Robot Car Chassis  

### Power Supply
- 8 × 1.5V AA batteries (motor driver and ESP32 VIN)  
- 2 × 18650 Li-ion batteries (ESP32 supply)  
- YwRobot MB102 Breadboard Power Supply Module (voltage regulator)

### Indicators and Output
- White LED (Front)  
- Red LED (Rear)  
- Buzzer  

### Miscellaneous
- Resistors  
- Jumper wires  
- Soldering wires and solder  
- Breadboard
